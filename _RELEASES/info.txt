Description
===========

Compatible with iOS 5,6 and 7

Boover lets you move your badges. Use pre-defined values, manual input or a mix of them both. Haven't we always wanted the badges 13 pixels up from the bottom right corner? With Boover this is finally possible!

Perfect for themers.

Configure options from Settings app.
